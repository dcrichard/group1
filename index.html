About me

Damien is originally from Mt. Desert Island, Maine; but, he grew up on the Central Oregon coast. In his early years he traveled across the United States extensively. By the age of 9 he had attended school in four different states (including Maine, Hawaii, Oregon, and Florida), settling in Oregon where he remained until he graduated from high school in 1993.


After graduating from high school he joined the US Army as a Heavy Wheel Vehicle Mechanic, completing both his Basic and Advanced training at Ft. Jackson, South Carolina. He spent three years in the military, each year at a different duty station. His first year was spent at Ft. Knox, Kentucky, his second year was spent near Soul, Korea, and his final year was spent at Ft. Stewart, Georgia, honorably completing his service obligation at the rank of Specialist. Damien thoroughly enjoyed his time serving the country and would do it over again if given the same choices.

Family

Damien is happily married to his wife Kelly, and has 5 children. As to be expected, their houshold is very active, although; with the two oldest children both now serving in the military itâ€™s not as busy around the house as it has been in the past. The children that are still in the house range from 16 years old down to 5 years old, and they are all active in sports and other extracurricular activities, so there never seems to be a dull moment or lack of functions to attend.

Occupation

After completing his tour in the military, he began working in the gaming industry, working two years at a casino on the Oregon coast. In 1998 he hired on with a gaming vendor based out of Lake Tahoe, and moved to Carson City, Nevada. In 2000 he moved to Reno, Nevada, where he has been living ever since. Today Damien works as a Senior Systems Software QA Manager for a global gaming company that designs, manufacturers, distributes, and operates gaming devices and computerized monitoring, accounting, and player-tracking systems for the gaming/lottery industries.

Hobbies

When Damien is not at work or taking classes he enjoys spending time with his family, fishing, watching football, and entertaining friends (not necessarily in that order).

Goals

Throughout Damien's life he has taken a considerable number of college courses, including web and application development; however, he has never earned a degree. His short term goal is to complete his Associates of Applied Science in Computer Information Technology with a Computer Programming Emphasis, then move forward towards a four year degree in the same area.

Project Subject
